Our Living Legacy

The Artisan Soul of Usiacurí

Usiacurí is recognized as the 'Manger of the Atlantic,' not only for its geography but for being the heart of the Iraca Palm weaving tradition. This ancestral craft, inherited from the Mokaná indigenous people, has transformed our town into a world-class center for sustainable fashion and cultural heritage

A Woven Journey

Carludovica Palmata

The Iraca Palm, or "Paja Toquilla," is the heart of our crafts. Artisans select, boil, and dry the fiber under the Atlantic sun to achieve its legendary ivory flexibility

Mokaná Mastery

A skill inherited from the Mokaná ancestors. Every piece uses complex interlocking patterns and natural dyes extracted from local minerals and plants

Sustainable Fashion

From the streets of Usiacurí to the catwalks of Milan. This eco-friendly craft supports over 70% of local families, merging heritage with luxury design
